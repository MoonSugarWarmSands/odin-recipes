Asaldak's Moon Candy

Asaldak's Moon Candy is a favourite amongst the Cathay-raht. So Asaldak has been told. I suspect they give it to the Alfiq when Asaldak is not around.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup from Sack of Flour
  • 1/4 tsp from Salt Pile
  • 1 stick (1/2 cup) of Butter
  • 3/4 cup of Honey
  • 1 1/2 tsp Extract from Vanilla Plant
  • 2 cups Moon Sugar

How-To

  1. Mix the dry recipe ingredients in a bowl. Set aside
  2. In a pan, mix the sugar and butter. Bring to a boil and stir occasionally. Check the mixture dropping a small spoonful into cold water. Reach into the water and try to form a ball from the syrup with your claws. It if stays together for a few seconds then it's ready
  3. When ready, remove from the heat right away and pour over the flour mixture. Mix well
  4. Add Extract from Vanilla Plant. Mix in well too
  5. Pour mixture into strips and leave to cool
