Asaldak's Special Skooma

So you have found Asaldak's hidden page... Jode and Jone smile upon you this day.
Asaldak will show you how to make skooma. Be warned.
These sugars would make a milk drinker spit on Shor's bones for another taste.
Kha'jay siirithse jer draqo.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Moon Sugar
  • 1 cup of boiling water
  • 5 small fruits of your choice
  • 1/4 tsp Extract from Vanilla Plant

How-To

  1. Combine Moon Sugar and boiling water
  2. Stir until Moon Sugar has dissolved
  3. Pour into a clean bottle and add fruits of your choice and the Extract from Vanilla Plant
  4. Store secretly in a dark room, bring out into the moonlight once everyday and shake
  5. Do this for 2 weeks at minimum, leave for longer for a more potent solution
  6. Strain remaining ingredients out and add to clean bottles for consumption
